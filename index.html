Biography

I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer focused on video applications at TikTok. I earned my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science in December 2023 from Duke University with a research focus on video motion tracking under the supervision of Prof. Carlo Tomasi. Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science in June 2018 from Nanjing University, where I did undergraduate research supervised by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou at NJU LAMDA Group.
